Privacy Policy for VoiceScribe

Last Updated: December 13, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to VoiceScribe ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username and password (stored locally on your device)
  • Dictation Content: Voice recordings converted to text, titles, categories, and notes you create
  • Preferences: App settings, theme preferences, and notification preferences

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for compatibility purposes
  • Usage Data: Features used, crash reports, and error logs to improve app stability

2.3 Voice Data

  • Voice recordings are processed using Android's built-in speech recognition or Google Cloud Speech-to-Text API
  • Voice audio is converted to text and NOT stored as audio files
  • Speech recognition happens on-device when using Android's built-in recognizer
  • When using cloud-based recognition, audio is sent to Google's servers following their privacy policy

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the VoiceScribe service
  • Convert your voice into text dictations
  • Sync your data with Google Sheets (if you enable this feature)
  • Send you reminders and notifications for your dictations
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage

4.1 Local Storage

  • All dictation data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • Your account credentials are stored locally using industry-standard encryption (BCrypt)
  • App preferences are stored in device secure storage

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • If you enable Google Sheets integration, your dictations are synced to your personal Google Sheets
  • You control access to this Google Sheet through your Google Account settings
  • We do not have access to your Google Sheets data

5. Third-Party Services

VoiceScribe integrates with the following third-party services:

5.1 Google Services

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication when using Google Sheets sync
  • Google Sheets API: For syncing your dictations (optional)
  • Google Drive API: For backup storage (optional)
  • Google Cloud Speech-to-Text: For voice recognition (optional)

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 Firebase Crashlytics (if implemented)

  • Collects crash reports and performance data
  • Does not collect personally identifiable information

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly choose to sync with Google Sheets
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the app (Google Cloud, etc.)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your dictation data within the app
  • Export: Export your data using the Backup feature
  • Delete: Delete individual dictations or your entire account
  • Opt-Out: Disable Google Sheets sync, reminders, or voice commands at any time

8. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are encrypted using BCrypt hashing
  • Data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Local data is protected by Android's app sandboxing
  • Google Sheets sync uses OAuth 2.0 authentication

9. Children's Privacy

VoiceScribe is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Data Retention

  • Your dictation data is stored indefinitely until you delete it
  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • Backup files are retained based on your storage preferences
  • Crash reports are retained for up to 90 days

11. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you use Google Sheets sync, your data may be transferred to and stored on Google's servers, which may be located outside your country. Google complies with applicable data protection laws.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for material changes

13. Your California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell your information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

14. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
